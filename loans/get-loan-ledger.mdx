---
title: "Get a loan's ledger"
description: "Returns the top 50 transactions in the ledger of the loan"
api: "GET /loan/{id}/transaction"
---


Returns the top 50 transactions in the ledger of the loan. Associated payment, person, trust, and charge details are included in each record if applicable.

## Path Parameters

<ParamField path="id" type="string">
  Loan ID
</ParamField>
